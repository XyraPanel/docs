## Support Expectations

XyraPanel is MIT-licensed and open source. **Support is not guaranteed.**

- Don’t spam or pressure maintainers
- Don’t demand fixes or responses
- Help is voluntary
- **Read the docs first**

We fix bugs and improve the software, not teach every step. Do basic research before asking for help.

## Expected Conduct

- Act professionally
- Be respectful and constructive
- No drama, harassment, or toxicity
- Take responsibility for your setup

## Security Vulnerabilities

**Do not report vulnerabilities publicly.**

- Use GitHub’s private security reports
- Provide clear details
- Don’t exploit or share vulnerabilities
- Allow time for a fix

## Reporting Issues

- Search existing issues first
- Provide steps, logs, and system info
- Don’t bump or spam

---

::warning
Failure to follow these standards may result in removal from community spaces.
::
