XyraPanel is a game management panel like **_[Pterodactyl](https://pterodactyl.io/)_**, except built with newer tech and community expectations in mind. The project exists because many of us were tired of slow updates, blocked community ideas, and outdated tooling while the rest of the internet proceeded with progression.
